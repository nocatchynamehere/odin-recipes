Tacos

Description:

A fun night of tacos is just a walk to the grocery store. This experience will bring your family together like never before. Clean shredded cheese off the floor for days to come.

Have some delicious tacos tonight!

Ingredients:

  • Taco shells from the grocery store
  • Ground beef from the grocery store
  • Shredded Cheese from the grocery store
  • Ortega sauce from the grocery store

Steps:

  1. Brown the beef in a saucepan
  2. Warm taco shells in oven
  3. Procure warm taco shell
  4. Place browned beef in taco shell
  5. Place Shredded Cheese in taco shell, on top of ground beef
  6. Pour Ortega sauce on top of taco
  7. Take a big, watch half of it fall on the floor
  8. Repeat too many times
